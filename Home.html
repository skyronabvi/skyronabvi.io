WELCOME TO COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL - MUBENDE

Services Offered:

  1. Secondary Education:
    • The school offers both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level) education, providing a wide range of subjects in sciences, arts, and humanities.
  2. Extracurricular Activities:
    • Sports programs, including football, netball, and athletics.
    • Clubs and societies such as debate, drama, music, and cultural groups.
  3. Guidance and Counseling:
    • Services aimed at supporting students' academic and personal development.
  4. Library and Resource Center:
    • Access to books, academic resources, and study materials to support learning.
  5. Laboratories:
    • Well-equipped science labs for practical lessons in subjects like biology, chemistry, and physics.
  6. Computer Lab:
    • Computer literacy programs and access to digital resources.
  7. Boarding Facilities:
    • Boarding services for students from distant locations, with separate dormitories for boys and girls.
  8. Religious Services:
    • Provision for spiritual growth through various religious activities and services.
