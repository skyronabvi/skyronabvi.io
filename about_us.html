WELCOME TO COMPREHENSIVE HIGH SCHOOL - MUBENDE

Comprehensive High School in Mubende is a leading educational institution dedicated to providing high-quality secondary education to students in Uganda. Located in the heart of Mubende, our school has established itself as a center of academic excellence, nurturing young minds to become responsible, skilled, and well-rounded individuals.

Our Vision: To be a beacon of excellence in secondary education, producing graduates who are academically proficient, morally upright, and equipped with the skills necessary to thrive in a dynamic world.

Our Mission: To provide a conducive learning environment that fosters academic achievement, character development, and the acquisition of life skills, enabling students to realize their full potential.

Core Values:

  • Integrity: Upholding honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our dealings.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in academics, extracurricular activities, and personal conduct.
  • Respect: Promoting a culture of respect for self, others, and the environment.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and the use of modern technology in learning.
  • Community: Fostering a sense of belonging and responsibility towards the school, the community, and the nation.

Academic Programs: Comprehensive High School offers a wide range of subjects at both Ordinary Level (O-Level) and Advanced Level (A-Level), with a focus on science, arts, and humanities. Our dedicated teaching staff are committed to delivering quality education through innovative teaching methods and continuous assessment.

Extracurricular Activities: We believe in the holistic development of our students, and therefore, offer various extracurricular activities such as sports, music, drama, debate, and cultural programs. These activities help students develop teamwork, leadership, and communication skills.

Facilities: Our school boasts modern facilities, including well-equipped science laboratories, a computer lab, a well-stocked library, and boarding facilities. We also have a strong emphasis on guidance and counseling services to support students' academic and personal growth.

Community Engagement: Comprehensive High School is deeply rooted in the Mubende community. We engage in various community service projects, promoting social responsibility and the spirit of giving back to society.

Conclusion: At Comprehensive High School - Mubende, we are committed to shaping the future of our students by providing them with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to succeed in life. We welcome students from diverse backgrounds and are dedicated to fostering an inclusive and supportive learning environment.
